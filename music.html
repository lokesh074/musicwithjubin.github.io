One of the best singer JUBIN NAUTIYAL

Jubin Nautiyal (born 14 June 1989) is an Indian playback singer and performer. He was awarded Upcoming Male Vocalist of the Year at 8th Mirchi Music Awards for his song "Zindagi Kuch Toh Bata (Reprise)" from Bajrangi Bhaijaan. He also won the Rising Musical Star Award (2015) at Zee Business Awards. He has since recorded songs for films in various Indian languages, predominantly Urdu, and has established himself as one of the most sensational singers in India.
